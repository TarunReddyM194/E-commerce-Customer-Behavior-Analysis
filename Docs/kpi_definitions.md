\# KPI Definitions



\## Total Orders



\* Definition: Total number of orders placed by customers.

\* Calculation: COUNT(order\_id)

\* Importance: Helps measure overall business activity.



\## Total Revenue



\* Definition: Total revenue generated from all orders.

\* Calculation: SUM(payment\_value)

\* Importance: Indicates overall business performance.



\## Total Customers



\* Definition: Number of unique customers.

\* Calculation: COUNT(DISTINCT customer\_id)

\* Importance: Helps understand customer base size.



\## Repeat Customer Rate



\* Definition: Percentage of customers who made more than one purchase.

\* Calculation: (Repeat Customers / Total Customers) \* 100

\* Importance: Measures customer retention.



\## Cart Abandonment Rate



\* Definition: Percentage of orders that were canceled.

\* Calculation: (Canceled Orders / Total Orders) \* 100

\* Importance: Indicates drop-off in purchase process.
