\# Data Dictionary



\## orders\_dataset



\* order\_id: Unique identifier for each order

\* customer\_id: Unique identifier for each customer

\* order\_status: Status of the order (delivered, shipped, etc.)

\* order\_purchase\_timestamp: Timestamp when order was placed



\## order\_items\_dataset



\* order\_id: Order identifier

\* product\_id: Product identifier

\* seller\_id: Seller identifier

\* price: Price of the product

\* freight\_value: Shipping cost



\## products\_dataset



\* product\_id: Unique product identifier

\* product\_category\_name: Category of the product



\## order\_payments\_dataset



\* order\_id: Order identifier

\* payment\_value: Total payment amount
